Mavis Legacy Foundation

Welcome to the Mavis Legacy Foundation! We are dedicated to making a positive impact in our community by supporting those in need.

Who We Donate To

  • Local Orphanages: Providing food, education, and shelter for children in need.
  • Health Clinics: Supporting access to medical supplies and care in underserved communities.
  • Educational Programs: Funding scholarships and school supplies for underprivileged students.
  • Community Food Banks: Ensuring families have access to nutritious meals.
